Welcome to FOE Statistics!

The purpose is to create an objective ranking of the special buildings and Great Monuments in Forge of Empires.

For this, the method is simple: to calculate an efficiency rate based on normal, common, or 1x1 buildings .

Remember that if you like a building, ultimately that's the most important thing. Here, we focus on providing a mathematical overview.

-------------------------------------------

The Great Buildings (GBs) have been based on their level 10 , which is a good compromise and may potentially be reviewed upwards by more advanced players.

The calculation age is set to Mars, which is neutral for age-dependent bonuses but devalues fixed bonuses. In other words, the ranking for an early-game player might be quite different.

A Benchmark is available. You can adjust it as you wish to give more or less importance to certain bonuses, and the calculations will be automatically updated.

Some calculations have specificities, which I list here for information:
  • Certain Unique Bonuses could not be calculated. The efficiency is then displayed in italic and orange. Thus, this efficiency is undervalued.
  • All "motivation" bonuses are considered acquired.
  • When the bonus is limited to N uses, the formula is: bonus x (log(N)/2) . The efficiency increases greatly with the bonus and slightly with the number of uses, decreasing as it gets higher.
  • Any random reward is replaced by its average.
  • Critical costs are calculated as an attack increase: chance x 0.5.
  • The Himeji Castle's building reward is included in its diamond reward.
  • The Tropical Forest gives twice as many plans at level 10. Considering that you obtain approximately 1 plan per day, this bonus is equivalent to 1 plan.
