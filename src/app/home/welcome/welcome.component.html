Welcome to FOE Statistics!

The purpose is to create an objective ranking of the special buildings and Great Monuments in Forge of Empires.

For this, the method is simple: to calculate an efficiency rate based on normal, common, or 1x1 buildings .

Remember that if you like a building, ultimately that's the most important thing. Here, we focus on providing a mathematical overview.

How to start

Data

This website store the building list on your browser cache. If you clear it, everything will be reset !

To start (and refresh data), press the arrow button on top-right. It will get data on the game wikia. It will take about one minute. Do not close the site while the arrow is blue !

The data is pulled from this google datasheet. Do not hesitate to contribute to it.

Visualisation

Each card describe a building statistics. The detailed values are unitary (for 1 tile of the building).

Difference between "Special" and "Event" buildings here is that Event buildings needs roads.

Certain Unique Bonuses could not be calculated. The efficiency is then displayed in italic and orange. Thus, this efficiency is undervalued.

Customization

A Benchmark is available. You can adjust it as you wish to give more or less importance to certain bonuses, and the calculations will be automatically updated.

Mathematical Choices

The Great Buildings (GBs) have been based on their level 40 , which is a good compromise and may potentially be reviewed upwards by more advanced players.

The calculation age is set to Space Age Titan, which is neutral for age-dependent bonuses but devalues fixed bonuses. In other words, the ranking for an early-game player might be a bit different.

Specifications :

  • All "motivation" bonuses are considered acquired.
  • Limited uses bonuses are considered acquired.
  • War tributes, diplomatic gifts and similar are considered acquired.
  • Any random reward is replaced by its average.
  • Criticals are calculated as an attack increase: chance x 0.5.
  • All Great Buildings with missing attribute are indicated with a *
  • The Tropical Forest estimation is calculated on an estimated base chance of 2%, with 100 daily interractions
  • Special goods are considered as 2 resources each (average cost of expedition)
